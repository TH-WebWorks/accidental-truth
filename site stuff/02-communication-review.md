## Phase Breakdown

### 1. Initial Scope Intake

Client requested:

* New website on accidentaltruths.com
* Pull content from existing MUFON page
* Add new film information
* Use supplied graphics and media
* One-page structure to start

No pricing or timeline discussed at this stage.

---

### 2. Proposal Issuance

T&H WebWorks response included:

* $750 flat rate quote
* Single-page build scope
* Collaborative design phase
* Thematic positioning aligned to documentary subject matter
* Estimated 2–3 day development window post-design approval

---

### 3. Scope Expansion Signals

Client introduced additional requirements:

* Merchandise embeds (Shopify / WooCommerce)
* Expandability considerations
* Hosting and domain structure questions
* MUFON WordPress integration inquiry

Scope complexity increased beyond original ask.

---

### 4. Technical Positioning

T&H WebWorks advised:

* Against building within MUFON WordPress environment
* Establishing standalone domain ownership
* SEO canonical optimization benefits
* Treating MUFON as distribution rather than primary property

This positioned T&H WebWorks as strategic advisor, not just implementer.

---

### 5. Payment & Access Activation

Client actions:

* Requested PayPal payment details
* Sent deposit
* Provided Directnic hosting access
* Initiated infrastructure collaboration

Project moved from pre-sale to active execution.

---

### 6. Execution Directives

Client provided operational task list:

* Bring domain live
* Pull existing MUFON content
* Add merchandise section
* Insert new film materials at top
* Await additional assets

---

### 7. Urgency Layer

Client communicated:

* Active promotional rollout
* Need for immediate web presence
* Acceptance of basic initial version

Timeline compression acknowledged.

---

## Process Observations

### Strengths

* Fast deal closure
* Early price anchoring
* Authority positioning
* Infrastructure ownership guidance
* Smooth payment transition

---

### Gaps Identified

**1. Scope Lock**

Price issued before full expansion surfaced.

**2. Phase Separation**

Discovery, build, commerce, and hosting blended in thread.

**3. Payment Structure**

Deposit informal rather than milestone-based.

**4. Asset Intake**

No structured asset delivery system at kickoff.

---

## Process Improvements Derived

Future projects should include:

* Written scope approval before quoting
* Phase-segmented pricing
* Deposit + final payment structure
* Asset checklist at kickoff
* Hosting questionnaire pre-build
