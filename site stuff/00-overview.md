---

## Business and Delivery Intent

- Establish `accidentaltruths.com` as the primary property for the documentary.
- Use MUFON as a distribution channel, not the canonical site experience.
- Launch quickly with a high-clarity single-page structure.
- Include a lightweight merchandise section without full e-commerce buildout.
- Keep the structure expansion-ready for future multi-page growth.

---

## In-Scope Deliverables (Phase 1)

- Single-page website build
- New film content section at top of page
- Original film content migrated from MUFON source material
- Merchandise embed section (Shopify/WooCommerce-ready, lightweight integration)
- Mobile-responsive implementation
- Basic SEO foundations (meta tags, Open Graph, canonical URL)
- Domain bring-live with DNS/SSL completion

---

## Out-of-Scope (Current Engagement)

- Full build inside MUFON WordPress environment
- Full e-commerce implementation (catalog, checkout architecture, fulfillment logic)
- Multi-page expansion in this initial release window
